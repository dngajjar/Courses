Mean Face

 

In this section, I generated the mean face based on the input image data set. Used the sets of 43 control points that students made for their faces, and calculated the "mean face" by averaging the sets of points together to calculate the average geometry indicated by the triangulation, morphing each face into that averaged geometry, and averaging the resulting colors together.
