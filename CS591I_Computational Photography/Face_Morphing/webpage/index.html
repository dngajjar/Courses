Face Morphing

 

A morph is a simultaneous warp of the image shape and a cross-dissolve of image colors. The warp is controlled by defining a correspondence between two or more images which can be done by labeling the corresponding points such as mapping eyes to eyes, nose to nose, mouth to mouth, etc., to cover the whole face and get smoothese transformations possible.

In this assignment, my work is as following:

1. I created a 2 seconds video to show the morphing process from one image to another.

2. I computed the mean face based on the provided database of 17 people and managed to do some manipulations.

3. I generated the PCA basis.
