Bad Results

 

The seam carving algorithm cannot be perfect for all images. Through choosing different structures of image and testing the seam carving algorithm, I figured out two major problems that can usually occur on the output images as following:

1). Distortion of lines or object's contour;

2). Mismatch of content color along the seam crossing path;

These two flaws can happen with all the experiments conducted through this project, and in the next section I presented the "bad" images I created with apparently artificial processing along the seam crossing path.
