Selection Editing

The Selection Editing Algorithm is a kind of in-place image transformations that the guidance field is entirely dependent on the source image. By defining different guidance field of the source image, I acquired several functions of local image selection editing, such as changing the illumination or flatening the content of the selected region and tiling the same image in a seamless way.
