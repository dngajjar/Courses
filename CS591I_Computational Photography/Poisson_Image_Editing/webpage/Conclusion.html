Conclusion

By defining different guidance fields, we can have a variety of tools to achieve local seamless editing on the selected region. The effects could be to replace or to mix with another source image,  to change some aspects of the original image, such as texture and illumination. Under some conditions, we can get some good results from these algorithms.

All the images used in the project are from google images.
